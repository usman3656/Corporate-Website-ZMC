Who are we?

ZM Corporation was estabilished in 1992. It started as a small supplier business which gradually evolved into one of the most reputable plastic bags manufacturers in the market due to the endless struggles and efforts of our CEO Mr. Abdul Ghani Bawany. Now we can proudly say that with the blessings of the Almighty we have emerged as a powerful brand and a market leader. The company has state of the art machinery with quality control laboratory. Our well developed infrastructure has all the required facilities to manufacture the most optimum product to cater the requirements of our clients.

What we do

ZMC is a dynamic business organization that manufactures a variety of plastic bags. We convert different types of substrates including BOPET, metalized-BOPET, Matt BOPP, Pearlized BOPP, BOPP, metalized-BOPP, CPP, metalized-CPP,PE and PP into two layer and three layer laminates. We also consider it our responsibility to do our part for the environment and offer biodegradable products .With outstanding quality, competitive pricing and customer satisfaction being the founding pillars of this company, we have progressively acquired an impressive number of respected companies among our clientele and aim to be the biggest business organization across Pakistan.
